About 7rectangles

The graphics are randomly generated, made by layering 7 rectangles of random size, color and transparency on either a white or black background (also randomly selected). It is your task to rate them according to how much they appeal to you. You will see (up to) 100 of these randomly generated compositions, and you can move to the next or previous one to see them again and perhaps adjust your rating.

Being random, it is interesting that any of these images appeal to us. Many or most are duds, for sure, but there are a few that surprise us and appeal to our sense of color or composition or balance. We can find some order and harmony in an random arrangement of rectangles, or we may like an off-kilter composition that speaks to us. It also shows us that what we would not have arranged our selves. It presents us something new, opening us to new aesthetic possibilities.

In the near future, you will see thumbnail images of your top rated compositions and you'll be able to publish galleries of these minor masterpieces. --Thanks for trying it out, we hope you've had some fun.
